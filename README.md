# RJ45aaS (RJ45 as a Service) 🔌

> **Status:** Production Ready. Mission Critical. 

Let's be honest. The cloud has taken everything from us. Sure, AWS, Azure, and GCP gave us infinite scalability, high availability, and global load balancing. But at what cost? 

They took away the physical sensation of networking. They took away the definitive, plastic *SNAP* of seating a Cat6 cable perfectly into a switch. They took away our joy.

**RJ45aaS** is an open-source, highly-available, serverless platform dedicated to restoring the dopamine hit of physical networking to the modern cloud engineer.

## Features
- **Tactile Emulation:** State-of-the-art CSS transforms simulating physical cable insertion.
- **High-Fidelity Audio:** A real `.mp3` file of a real RJ45 clip clicking into a real port. No AI-generated fake sounds here. Pure analog plastic-on-plastic audio.
- **Infinite Scalability:** You can click the button as many times as your browser's memory leak limits allow.
- **Zero-Trust Network:** Actually does zero networking. Totally secure.

## Quick Start
1. Go to the GitHub Pages link.
2. Stare at the empty port.
3. Click "Insert Cable".
4. Feel the rush of 1999 flow through your veins.
5. Unplug and repeat until your imposter syndrome fades.
